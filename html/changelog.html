08 May 2024

updated:

  • Adds a minigun sound to the mounted and handheld miniguns. The sound is "Vulcan Machine Gun Series" from the YouTube Sound Library and part of Creative Commons (CC BY 4.0). Edited by me in Audacity to be a looping 0.5 second .ogg file.
  • Applies the gunshot_smg sound effect to the sec_smg.
  • Hivebots have had projectilesound added to some entries as the projectiles they were using no longer have their own sounds.
  • Moves most ballistic weapon sounds to the guns instead of them being weapon specific.
  • Adds a missing neckbrace to the GCC operative hardsuit that now shows when active.

30 April 2024

emmanuelbassil updated:

  • Can wrap boxes again.
  • Fixed being able to wrap items in storage containers, breaking the entire interaction.
  • Fixes gun-bashing. Clicking on someone while on harm intent with safety enabled will bash with the gun.

rootoo807 updated:

  • Ports new sprites for cans, bottles, condiments, other assorted reagent containers from Aurora. Updates several unique Bay sprites to the same standard.
  • Soda cans and other closed drinks can be shaken (use on harm intent). If shaken, fizzy drinks explode when opened.
  • Adds champagne popping and sabrage (opening with swords). Sabrage needs experienced cooking skill for a 100% success chance.
  • Adds ground peanuts (obtained by growing peanuts) and almond meal (obtained by growing almonds). Added these to several food items and a new protein bar flavor, "peanut crunch".
  • Adds peanut butter and "NTella", recipes for making them, and several new food and drink recipes that use them (ported from Aurora, PBJ sprite from tg).
  • Adds recipes for sprinkles and white wine.
  • Vacuum flasks have caps now. You can take them off and use them as cups.
  • Shakers have sounds when shaken.
  • Coffee cups (the ones from vending machines), cup ramen (resprited), paper water cups, and condiment containers (the generic ones) show the color of their contents. Tweaked some reagent colors to look nicer in cups.
  • Soda bottles are now small bottles to match their new sprites, doubled the amount of soda bottles in the booze-o-mat to compensate.
